Our Heritage

Woven from the
Soul of Assam

Assamese weaving is one of India's most storied textile traditions, stretching back thousands of years. Our sarees are sourced directly from master weavers of Sualkuchi — the "Manchester of the East" — where the art of muga and pat silk has been passed down through generations.

Every saree at Mughashree is a living piece of cultural heritage: hand-woven on traditional pit looms, dyed with natural colours, and finished with the signature motifs of Assamese folk art — peacocks, elephants, and the iconic kingkhap border.
